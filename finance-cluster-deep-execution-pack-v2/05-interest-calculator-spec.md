- total interest
- final amount
- monthly profit estimate

## Inputs

- principal (toman)
- annual rate (%)
- duration (months)

  - compound (optional, only if you can explain clearly)

## Outputs

- interest (toman)
- final amount (toman)
- monthly profit (toman)
